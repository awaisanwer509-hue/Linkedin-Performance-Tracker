LinkedIn Auditing Guide

Step 1: Ask Client to Export Data

Clients can easily export reports directly from their LinkedIn account. No login sharing is required. Ask them to:

  1. Navigate to their own profile page.
  2. Click Analytics & Creator Hub -> Content -> Export (top right).
  3. Similarly, click Followers -> Export.

Step 2: Upload or Input in Hub

Upload those files inside the Setup Client workspace step. The parser will read their exact impressions and comments trends instantly.

Step 3: Generate PDF or Share Link

View the metrics and click Share Report to get a secure read-only URL you can send to your client, or print it out as a high-fidelity PDF sheet.
